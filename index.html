Haunted Hangman

Instructions:

    Guess a letter:
  1. Start by guessing any letter you think might be in the hidden word. You may only guess one letter at at time
    Check the word:
  1. If the letter you guessed is in the word, it will be added to the correct place in the word
  2. If the letter you guessed isn't in the the word, part of the hangman's body will appear
Win or Lose:
  • Win: You win if you can guess the entire word before the hangman's body is completely drawn
  • Lose: You lose the game if the hangman's body is completely drawn before you can fully guess the word or you guess wrong four times
    Keep Guessing:
      Continue guessing letters one at a time. Each time you guess wrong, more of the hangman figure will appear
